# Sword Coast

## Overview
A major western coastal corridor defined by maritime trade, rival city-states, and constant movement of people, coin, and conflict.

## Geographic Frame
- Runs along the western edge of Faerûn, joining sea lanes to inland caravan roads.
- Travel often pivots between harbor politics and dangerous overland stretches.
- The coast functions as both marketplace and military frontier.

## Political Context
- No single power controls the full coastline for long.
- City interests, mercantile leagues, and naval strength matter more than formal borders.
- Regional stability depends on negotiated access to ports and roads.

## At-the-Table Travel Use
- Use sea routes for fast movement with piracy, weather, and customs risks.
- Use the [[regions/trade-way|Trade Way]] for caravan missions, escorts, and toll disputes.
- Treat each major port as a different legal and faction environment.

## Campaign Themes
Trade wars, piracy, espionage, migration pressure, and alliance-making between unequal powers.

## Regional Pressure Points
- Harbor blockades and privateering escalation.
- Refugee waves after frontier raids or urban unrest.
- Merchant-charter conflicts that can trigger interstate incidents.

## Hooks
- A shipping compact collapses, and neutral agents are hired to prevent open conflict between two port authorities.
- A convoy carrying relief supplies vanishes between cities, with every faction blaming a different rival.
- A forged harbor charter threatens to invalidate months of contracts and ignite street unrest.

# Amnian Coast

## Overview
The Amnian Coast is the maritime face of [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]]: port chains, customs stations, convoy routes, and contested docking rights.

## Strategic Value
- controls access between inland Amnian markets and sea-lane trade
- concentrates tariff revenue and anti-smuggling enforcement
- attracts pirates, privateers, and covert commercial warfare

## Key Settlements
- [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]]
- [[settlements/murann|Murann]]

## Campaign Themes
Shipping sabotage, convoy defense, embargo politics, customs corruption, and maritime intelligence operations.

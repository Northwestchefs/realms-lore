# Unapproachable East

## Overview
A high-volatility eastern theater where competing states, magical deterrence, and long-distance trade routes collide.

## Campaign Themes
Proxy conflict, magical containment, border intelligence, caravan warfare, and hard-edged diplomacy.

## Route and Chokepoint Play
- Coastal pressure through [[regions/sea-of-fallen-stars|Sea of Fallen Stars]] shipping lanes.
- Overland leverage through caravan hubs and customs forts in [[nations-realms/thesk|Thesk]].
- Border deterrence tied to fear of [[landmarks/thaymount|Thaymount]] and covert escalation.

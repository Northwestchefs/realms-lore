# Western Heartlands

## Overview
A central overland region where caravan routes, agricultural zones, and political borders create persistent low-intensity conflict.

## Major Polities
- [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]]
- [[nations-realms/cormyr|Cormyr]]
- [[nations-realms/tethyr|Tethyr]]

## Amn Pressure Zones
- coastal-commercial pull from the [[regions/amnian-coast|Amnian Coast]]
- road and toll friction along the [[regions/trade-way|Trade Way]]
- competition for caravan security contracts and customs authority

## Campaign Themes
Border disputes, toll politics, noble rivalry, logistics-focused adventures, and trade-law enforcement.

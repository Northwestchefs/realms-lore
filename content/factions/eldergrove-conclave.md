# Eldergrove Conclave

## Overview
The Eldergrove Conclave is the civic government of [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], balancing druidic stewardship, star-omen governance, and practical district administration. It is respected less as a throne and more as a long-memory institution trusted to keep the city alive across centuries.

## Government Structure

### Seats and Chambers
- **Rootspeakers:** Elder druids who steward food cycles, sacred groves, and long-term ecological law.
- **Star Sages:** Astral interpreters who guide timing for major policy, diplomacy, and ritual obligations.
- **Civic Keepers:** Magistrates and wardens who handle trade petitions, tunnel security, and emergency response.

### Decision Pattern
- Most policy is drafted in district circles first, then raised to the Conclave for binding accords.
- Urgent threats can trigger a **Moonline Session**, allowing temporary decrees until the next full assembly.
- Precedent matters: old rulings are stored as sung star-chants and crystal-records, then cited in disputes.

## Operational Priorities
- Protect Callidae's concealment wards and hidden tunnel network.
- Prevent ecological collapse in the cavern biomes that sustain the city.
- Keep Rootmarket open enough for survival trade without exposing the city to hostile powers.
- Resolve district feuds before they fracture inter-enclave alliances.

# Shadow Thieves (Athkatla)

## Overview
A major underworld network active in Athkatla, balancing smuggling, protection rackets, intelligence brokerage, and covert contract work.

## Operating Pattern
- infiltrates legal trade through debt, intimidation, and corrupt intermediaries
- sells information to elites while preserving deniable distance
- competes and bargains with both civic authorities and rival criminal actors

## Campaign Use
Use this faction for informant chains, stolen-ledger plots, retaliation arcs, and uneasy alliances against worse threats.

## Canon Notes
Leadership and territorial control vary by timeline and source. Keep structure flexible and focus on network behavior.

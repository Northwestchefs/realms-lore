# Chasm Vulture

## Overview
Chasm Vulture is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Environment-specific Predator.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 3-8 around vertical routes.
- **Primary Terrain:** Pit chambers and rope-bridge zones in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Follows cleanup crews backed by [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Feathers and old rope fragments near ledges.
- **Tactics:** Waits for knockdown effects, then dives.
- **Negotiation:** Scatters from bright magic bursts.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Carrion heaps hide dropped rings and keys.

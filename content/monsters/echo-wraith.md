# Echo Wraith

## Overview
Echo Wraith is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Elite.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Solo or paired with skeletons.
- **Primary Terrain:** Silent audience halls beneath [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Sometimes unleashed by failed digs funded from [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Whispered fragments of old commands in empty rooms.
- **Tactics:** Targets low-Constitution creatures first to force retreat.
- **Negotiation:** Can be redirected by unresolved burial vows.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Ghost-marked medals tied to ancient watch captains.

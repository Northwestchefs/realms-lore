# Gloomknife Hexer

## Overview
Gloomknife Hexer is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Spellcaster.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 1 with 4-8 gloomknife goblins.
- **Primary Terrain:** Shrine alcoves around cracked wards in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Trades charms with [[factions/cult-of-the-dragon|Cult of the Dragon]] couriers.

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Ash circles and whispered counting in Goblin.
- **Tactics:** Opens with hold person then orders skirmishers to focus fire.
- **Negotiation:** Will barter prisoners for spell components.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Ritual tokens tied to deeper cult routes.

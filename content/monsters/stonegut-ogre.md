# Stonegut Ogre

## Overview
Stonegut Ogre is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Brute.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 1-2 with smaller skirmishers.
- **Primary Terrain:** Collapsed gatehouses inside [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Hired as muscle by smugglers from [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Crushed doors and drag marks from heavy crates.
- **Tactics:** Breaks cover and forces open party strongpoints.
- **Negotiation:** Accepts food and clear terms more than coin.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Stolen goods bundles labeled for dock pickup.

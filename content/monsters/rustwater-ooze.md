# Rustwater Ooze

## Overview
Rustwater Ooze is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Environment-specific Predator.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 2-4 in flooded corridors.
- **Primary Terrain:** Drain channels under [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Alchemists from [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]] secretly harvest samples.

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Smoking puddles and dissolved hinges.
- **Tactics:** Slides to isolate armored targets and melt gear carriers.
- **Negotiation:** Lured by fresh meat thrown into side drains.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Acid vials and half-dissolved lockboxes nearby.

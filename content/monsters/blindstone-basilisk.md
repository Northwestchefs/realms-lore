# Blindstone Basilisk

## Overview
Blindstone Basilisk is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Environment-specific Predator.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Single apex hazard.
- **Primary Terrain:** Mineral chambers near old quarries in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Sold as living trap beasts by criminal handlers in [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Statues with recent tool marks and drag channels.
- **Tactics:** Holds choke points and forces line-of-sight puzzles.
- **Negotiation:** Can be baited with mirrored decoys and loud bait animals.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Partially petrified gear and quarry records.

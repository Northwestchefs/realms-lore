# Deep Lamprey Swarm

## Overview
Deep Lamprey Swarm is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Strange Local Nuisance.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Best as hazard layered onto another fight.
- **Primary Terrain:** Flooded cisterns linked to [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Used by smugglers to guard submerged stashes.

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Water trembling without visible current.
- **Tactics:** Clings to wounded targets while bigger threats press.
- **Negotiation:** Can be distracted with slaughtered livestock.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Smuggler cache jars bolted above the waterline.

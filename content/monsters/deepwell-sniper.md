# Deepwell Sniper

## Overview
Deepwell Sniper is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Ranged Threat.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 2-3 with melee blockers.
- **Primary Terrain:** Collapsed balconies overlooking main descent routes in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Frequently contracted by [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Bolts fletched with black-and-red thread.
- **Tactics:** Focuses healers and light bearers first.
- **Negotiation:** Will accept payoff to misdirect rivals.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Ledger scraps tied to bounty payments.

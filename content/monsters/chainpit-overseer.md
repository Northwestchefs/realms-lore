# Chainpit Overseer

## Overview
Chainpit Overseer is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Leader.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 1 with 4-8 mixed troops.
- **Primary Terrain:** Slave pits and work camps in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Sells labor and loot channels to [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Iron spikes marked with tally runes.
- **Tactics:** Pins one target while minions collapse around it.
- **Negotiation:** Can be bought off with proof of profitable routes.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Work logs naming buyers in [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

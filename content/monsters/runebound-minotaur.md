# Runebound Minotaur

## Overview
Runebound Minotaur is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Elite.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Solo or with two skirmishers.
- **Primary Terrain:** Ancient ward halls in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Bound by failed mercenary contracts from [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Carved wall runes burned fresh with blood.
- **Tactics:** Charges narrow lanes to break formations fast.
- **Negotiation:** Can be stalled by presenting old maze sigils.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Rune plates and mercenary contract seals.

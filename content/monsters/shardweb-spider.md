# Shardweb Spider

## Overview
Shardweb Spider is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Ranged Threat.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 2 with goblin cutters or one as solo trap.
- **Primary Terrain:** Crystal caverns branching from [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Silk sold through [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]] cloth fronts.

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Reflective web strands across floor-level rubble.
- **Tactics:** Starts with Web Shot, then drags restrained prey upward.
- **Negotiation:** Can be driven off with loud resonance effects.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Shard silk bundles and cocooned satchels.

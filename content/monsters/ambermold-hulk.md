# Ambermold Hulk

## Overview
Ambermold Hulk is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Brute.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Pair with tunnel lurkers or goblin skirmishers.
- **Primary Terrain:** Collapsed fungal halls in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Often herded toward intruders by [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]] scouts.

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Rot-sweet fungus smell and drifting yellow dust.
- **Tactics:** Walks forward to lock choke points while allies flank.
- **Negotiation:** Can be distracted with fresh carrion or alchemical fire bait.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Amber resin nodules worth coin to apothecaries.

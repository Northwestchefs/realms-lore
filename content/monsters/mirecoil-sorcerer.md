# Mirecoil Sorcerer

## Overview
Mirecoil Sorcerer is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Spellcaster.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Solo with melee screen.
- **Primary Terrain:** Flooded ritual wells beneath [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Takes contracts from [[factions/kraken-society|Kraken Society]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Wet chalk spirals and copper bowls by drains.
- **Tactics:** Zones corridors with weather spells before retreating.
- **Negotiation:** Will bargain for rare pearls and safe escort.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Storm-etched tablets pointing to surface agents.

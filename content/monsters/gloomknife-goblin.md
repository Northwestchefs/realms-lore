# Gloomknife Goblin

## Overview
Gloomknife Goblin is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Skirmisher.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 3-6 with one leader.
- **Primary Terrain:** Side galleries near smuggler routes to [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Sells information to [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Tiny chalk arrows and stolen lamp oil caches.
- **Tactics:** Hit-and-run archery, then collapse onto isolated casters.
- **Negotiation:** Accepts coin for safe passage through trapped lanes.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Map scraps of nearby ladders and hidden lifts.

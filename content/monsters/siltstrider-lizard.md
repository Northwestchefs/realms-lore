# Siltstrider Lizard

## Overview
Siltstrider Lizard is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Skirmisher.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 4-8 as roaming pack.
- **Primary Terrain:** Mud channels and cistern shelves in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Captured as pets by gangs near [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Parallel claw marks in wet silt.
- **Tactics:** Circles for flanks and retreats from bright light.
- **Negotiation:** Food and open exits can divert the pack.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Leashed harnesses indicate nearby handlers.

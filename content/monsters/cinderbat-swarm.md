# Cinderbat Swarm

## Overview
Cinderbat Swarm is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Environment-specific Predator.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Hazard wing with one controller.
- **Primary Terrain:** Warm vent shafts under [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Captured by rogue mages tied to [[factions/arcane-brotherhood|Arcane Brotherhood]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Soot flecks and sudden gusts of hot air.
- **Tactics:** Fills spaces to punish clustered parties.
- **Negotiation:** Avoids open flame larger than a torch line.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Guano deposits useful for alchemy.

# Chokepoint Hook Horror

## Overview
Chokepoint Hook Horror is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Brute.
- **Suggested Grouping:** One with smaller scavengers.
- **Primary Terrain:** Bridge tunnels and pit edges in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Sometimes trapped and sold by [[factions/bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'aerthe]] crews.

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Metal-scrape clicks echoing in rhythm.
- **Tactics:** Starts with Hook from reach then corners one target.
- **Negotiation:** Retreats from loud thunder magic.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Bones and dropped packs in old nests.

# Vaultscale Tyrant

## Overview
Vaultscale Tyrant is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Leader.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Boss with 2-4 support monsters.
- **Primary Terrain:** Ancient vault intersections deep in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Controls tribute routes contested by [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]] and [[factions/bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'aerthe]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Tribute piles and disciplined patrol markings.
- **Tactics:** Uses fear aura to split parties, then focuses isolated targets.
- **Negotiation:** Will parley with groups offering tribute and maps.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Ledger tablets listing monsters and gangs paying tribute.

None.

Reactions

None.

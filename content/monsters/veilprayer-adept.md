# Veilprayer Adept

## Overview
Veilprayer Adept is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Spellcaster.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 1 with skeleton escorts.
- **Primary Terrain:** Defiled shrine circles in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Often aligned with [[factions/cult-of-the-dragon|Cult of the Dragon]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Wax-sealed prayers pinned to old stone idols.
- **Tactics:** Locks down one frontliner while minions surround.
- **Negotiation:** May trade information for relic recovery.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Cult route maps toward deeper vaults.

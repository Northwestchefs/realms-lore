# Gravewire Skeleton

## Overview
Gravewire Skeleton is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Ranged Threat.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 4-10 supporting necromancer encounters.
- **Primary Terrain:** Guard galleries near forgotten tomb doors in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Often controlled by cells linked to [[factions/cult-of-the-dragon|Cult of the Dragon]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Copper wire wrapping old bones.
- **Tactics:** Hold distance and focus one marked target.
- **Negotiation:** No negotiation without controller present.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Command sigils that point toward hidden crypt rooms.

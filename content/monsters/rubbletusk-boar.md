# Rubbletusk Boar

## Overview
Rubbletusk Boar is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Brute.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 2-3 with handlers.
- **Primary Terrain:** Collapsed market halls below [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Sometimes released by extortionists tied to [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Broken stone, rooting trenches, and crushed crates.
- **Tactics:** Opens with charge chains to scatter front lines.
- **Negotiation:** Can be calmed briefly with feed sacks and smoke.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Crate fragments marked with smuggler symbols.

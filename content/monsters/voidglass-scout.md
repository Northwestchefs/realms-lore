# Voidglass Scout

## Overview
Voidglass Scout is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Skirmisher.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 2-5 in advance teams.
- **Primary Terrain:** Lightless side roads near old drow outposts in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Competes with [[factions/bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'aerthe]] couriers.

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Black-glass arrowheads near scout posts.
- **Tactics:** Harasses rear ranks and retreats behind cover.
- **Negotiation:** Will exchange route intel for safe passage.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Notes about rival patrol timings.

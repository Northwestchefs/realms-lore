# Lanternmoth Cloud

## Overview
Lanternmoth Cloud is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Strange Local Nuisance.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Use as visibility hazard with another encounter.
- **Primary Terrain:** Phosphorescent fungus groves in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Collected by alchemists tied to [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Blue glow drifting against airflow.
- **Tactics:** Obscures lines of sight and protects ambushers.
- **Negotiation:** Torch smoke can push the cloud aside.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Luminous glands worth modest alchemy coin.

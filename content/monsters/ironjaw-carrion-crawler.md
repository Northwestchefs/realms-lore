# Ironjaw Carrion Crawler

## Overview
Ironjaw Carrion Crawler is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Brute.
- **Suggested Grouping:** 1-2 with scavenger swarms.
- **Primary Terrain:** Waste tunnels feeding lower chambers of [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Sometimes driven toward enemies by desperate gangs.

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Long slime trails with metal fragments embedded.
- **Tactics:** Paralyzes one target then drags it into darkness.
- **Negotiation:** Fresh offal can redirect it for one round.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Corroded armor pieces in nest piles.

# Saltfang Drake

## Overview
Saltfang Drake is a recurring threat in [[landmarks/undermountain|Undermountain]] that DMs can drop into patrols, lairs, or travel attrition scenes under [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Environment-specific Predator.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Single predator or mated pair.
- **Primary Terrain:** Brine cisterns linked to harbor channels below [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Egg clutches targeted by [[factions/kraken-society|Kraken Society]].

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Salt crust, fish bones, and frozen puddles underground.
- **Tactics:** Ambushes from water then retreats to force pursuit.
- **Negotiation:** Will disengage if eggs are threatened.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Pearl-like eggs and smuggled brine maps.

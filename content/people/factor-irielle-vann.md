# Factor Irielle Vann

## Overview
Temple-broker of [[faiths/waukeen|Waukeen]] who arbitrates caravan insurance and high-value cargo bonds across Sembian ports.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Trade arbitrator, lender, and contract enforcer.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[faiths/waukeen|Waukeen]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Holds leverage with counting houses in [[settlements/selgaunt|Selgaunt]] and financiers in [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]].
- **Base of Operations:** Golden Ledger Exchange, [[settlements/selgaunt|Selgaunt]].

## Description
- Dresses in travel-stained silk with stitched merchant seals from half the Inner Sea.
- Always carries a wax-sealed tube of emergency credit notes.

## History / Background
- Began as a dockside appraiser and built her influence by predicting piracy losses better than guild accountants.
- Publicly praises open markets while quietly blacklisting violent clients through temple channels.

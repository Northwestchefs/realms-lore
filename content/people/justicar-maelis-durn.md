# Justicar Maelis Durn

## Overview
Senior circuit-judge of [[faiths/tyr|Tyr]] who rides between market towns to hear petitions no lord wants on the record.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Itinerant magistrate, oath-witness, and legal mediator.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[faiths/tyr|Tyr]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Coordinates with Purple Dragon officers and consults [[factions/war-wizards|War Wizards]] for magical evidence.
- **Base of Operations:** Hall of Even Scales, [[settlements/suzail|Suzail]].

## Description
- Keeps meticulous court ribbons color-coded by severity of offense.
- Wears a steel gauntlet on her left hand as a visible symbol of judgment.

## History / Background
- Rose from junior temple scribe to royal circuit authority after exposing forged wartime pardons.
- Travels with a rotating escort to reduce local political pressure and bribery attempts.

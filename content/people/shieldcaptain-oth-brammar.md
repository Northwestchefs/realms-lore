# Shieldcaptain Oth Brammar

## Overview
Veteran Helmite officer who organizes temple-backed convoy shields on the [[regions/trade-way|Trade Way]] and treats route security like sacred duty.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Convoy marshal, militia trainer, and shrine quartermaster.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[faiths/helm|Helm]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Contracts with merchant costers in [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]] and city wardens in [[settlements/esmeltaran|Esmeltaran]].
- **Base of Operations:** Vigilant Bastion, [[settlements/esmeltaran|Esmeltaran]].

## Description
- Broad-shouldered dwarf with a mirror-bright helm and a caravan map etched on his shield rim.
- Speaks in clipped command phrases even during prayer.

## History / Background
- Survived three notorious ambush seasons and rebuilt his command around layered watch rotations.
- Known for refusing profitable escort contracts if clients insist on cutting rations for guards.

# Caelith Orreryn

## Overview
Caelith Orreryn serves as Callidae's Star Ledger Chancellor, translating celestial omens into policy calendars, legal thresholds, and diplomatic windows.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Star Sage administrator and keeper of Conclave precedent.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/eldergrove-conclave|Eldergrove Conclave]].
- **Base of Operations:** [[regions/callidae-districts/astral-observatory|Astral Observatory]].

## Description
- Crystal-lensed circlet and ink-stained gloves from constant chartwork.
- Keeps a rotating halo of tiny rune plates that mark unresolved civic cases.

## Personality
- Precise, dry-humored, and quietly compassionate.
- Distrusts quick answers to old problems.

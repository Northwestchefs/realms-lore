# Magister Selene Dawnscript

## Overview
Battlefield arcanist-priestess of [[faiths/mystra|Mystra]] who manages dangerous relic quarantine and emergency spell triage in the North.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Arcane investigator, shrine scholar, and disaster response coordinator.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[faiths/mystra|Mystra]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Consults with wardens in [[nations-realms/neverwinter|Neverwinter]] and mages in [[settlements/silverymoon|Silverymoon]].
- **Base of Operations:** Starveil Annex, [[settlements/silverymoon|Silverymoon]].

## Description
- Silver ink stains mark her fingers and robe cuffs from constant ward-scribing.
- Keeps crystal vials of stabilized Weave residue on a chain across her chest.

## History / Background
- Earned regional trust after containing a collapsing portal circle before it consumed a market square.
- Trains mixed teams of acolytes and scouts to identify arcane contamination quickly.

# Thane Korvul Icevein

## Overview
Gruff war-priest of [[faiths/tempus|Tempus]] who leads winter musters and teaches border militias how to survive berserker shock tactics.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Muster chaplain, siege advisor, and commander of volunteer shield circles.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[faiths/tempus|Tempus]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Coordinates with lodge captains near [[settlements/immilmar|Immilmar]] and caravan guards entering from [[nations-realms/thesk|Thesk]].
- **Base of Operations:** Frost-Banner Yard, [[settlements/immilmar|Immilmar]].

## Description
- Pale braided beard hung with carved bone tally-markers from past campaigns.
- Laughs loudly before battle, then goes silent once steel is drawn.

## History / Background
- Former front-rank champion who shifted into command after a leg wound ended his skirmisher career.
- Maintains practical alliances with non-Rashemi mercenaries when threats rise along contested roads.

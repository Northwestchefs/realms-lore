# Thamior Glassward

## Overview
Thamior Glassward commands the Conclave's civic wardens, coordinating tunnel patrols, ward integrity teams, and crisis response during incursions.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Warden-Captain and security voice of the Conclave.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/eldergrove-conclave|Eldergrove Conclave]].
- **Base of Operations:** Watch bastions between [[regions/callidae-districts/crystal-canopy|Crystal Canopy]] and [[regions/callidae-districts/moonwell-terraces|Moonwell Terraces]].

## Description
- Star-metal scale cloak over a sentinel's harness set with resonance crystals.
- Maps danger by sound and vibration, not just sight.

## Personality
- Vigilant, duty-first, and unexpectedly gentle with civilians.
- Suspicious of grand speeches and fond of actionable plans.

## Relationships
- [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]]
- [[factions/eldergrove-conclave|Eldergrove Conclave]]
- [[people/mireth-starlance|Mireth Starlance]]

## Plot Hooks
- Thamior recruits specialists to test a newly restored ward corridor after scouts vanish inside it.
- A string of false alarm runes threatens to break public trust in the warden network.

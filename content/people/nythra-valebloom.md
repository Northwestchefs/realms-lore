# Nythra Valebloom

## Overview
Nythra Valebloom is the First Rootspeaker of [[factions/eldergrove-conclave|Eldergrove Conclave]], a patient but unyielding civic druid who treats government as an act of cultivation, not command.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** First Rootspeaker and presiding voice in full Conclave assemblies.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/eldergrove-conclave|Eldergrove Conclave]].
- **Base of Operations:** Council rings in [[regions/callidae-districts/eldergrove|Eldergrove]].

## Description
- Wears layered bark-silk robes stitched with silver root patterns that shift with moonlight.
- Speaks softly in public councils, forcing even rivals to lean in and listen.

## Personality
- Deliberate, empathetic, and strategically conservative.
- Believes every decree should be survivable for seven generations.

## Relationships
- [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]]
- [[factions/eldergrove-conclave|Eldergrove Conclave]]
- [[people/orinath-starfallow|Orinath Starfallow]]

## Plot Hooks
- Nythra seeks neutral agents to investigate forged omen records before a legitimacy crisis erupts.
- A dissident district accuses her of hoarding water rites, and she needs proof before civil trust fractures.

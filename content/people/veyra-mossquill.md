# Veyra Mossquill

## Overview
Veyra Mossquill is the Petition Marshal of Callidae, the official most citizens actually meet when law, trade, or district conflict becomes urgent.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Civic Keeper overseeing hearings, contracts, and emergency writs.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/eldergrove-conclave|Eldergrove Conclave]].
- **Base of Operations:** Hall of Petitions in [[regions/callidae-districts/rootmarket|Rootmarket]].

## Description
- Carries a lacquered case of living reed-scrolls that rewrite themselves as testimony changes.
- Maintains impeccable courtroom calm even when tempers and spores both run hot.

## Personality
- Pragmatic, fair-minded, and relentlessly prepared.
- Sees compromise as a craft rather than a weakness.

## Relationships
- [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]]
- [[regions/callidae-districts/rootmarket|Rootmarket]]

## Plot Hooks
- Veyra asks adventurers to escort a controversial witness through tunnel routes before sabotage can silence them.

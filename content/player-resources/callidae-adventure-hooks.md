# Adventure Hooks of Callidae

## Garden Mysteries

### Hook 1
A moonpetal bed in [[regions/callidae-districts/starlight-gardens|Starlight Gardens]] blooms in reverse order, and the druids suspect someone is rewriting seasonal rites.

### Hook 2
A baby unicorn vanished near [[landmarks/callidae/silverleaf-unicorn-glade|Silverleaf Unicorn Glade]], leaving only silver hoofprints that stop at a sealed hedge wall.

### Hook 3
The gardeners of [[landmarks/sapmoon-greenhouse|Sapmoon Greenhouse]] report that every healing herb now carries mild dream-poison.

### Hook 4
A sacred topiary has grown into the exact shape of a missing elder, and no one admits to carving it.

### Hook 5
Bees from the starhoney hives no longer sting intruders but attack ordained druids on sight.

### Hook 6
An apprentice seed-keeper sold counterfeit glyph-tags in [[regions/callidae-districts/rootmarket|Rootmarket]], and rare vines are mutating across private courtyards.

### Hook 7
Every dawn, a different fruit tree uproots itself and walks closer to [[regions/callidae-districts/eldergrove|Eldergrove]].

### Hook 8
A medicinal moss bed whispers prophecies in a dead dialect whenever moonlight touches it.

### Hook 9
The irrigation channels carry glittering spores that erase memories of the previous day.

### Hook 10
A ceremonial orchard map no longer matches the living grove, as if paths are changing overnight.

### Hook 11
Predatory vine-cats have appeared in nursery plots and drag off seed satchels instead of prey.

### Hook 12
A dryad asks the party to prove she is the true spirit of her tree before council clerks reassign her grove.

### Hook 13
Someone stole pollen from a quarantine bloom that should only open during eclipses.

### Hook 14
A druid circle feud turns violent after one side claims the other is grafting abyssal roots into public gardens.

### Hook 15
The soil around a children's learning grove has turned to warm glass after a meteor shower.

### Hook 16
A giant stag made of woven branches appears at dusk and challenges passersby to riddles about old treaties.

### Hook 17
Seed registries at [[landmarks/keeper-of-seeds-pavilion|Keeper of Seeds Pavilion]] show a crop that was never planted but is now fully grown.

### Hook 18
Harvest workers discovered a tunnel beneath a herb terrace lined with petrified druid masks.

### Hook 19
The ward-ivy on a healer's home has gone dormant, and someone is stalking patients between moonwell visits.

### Hook 20
A blossom that cures curses now opens only when fed blood from a willing noble house heir.

## Fey Intrigue

### Hook 21
A delegation from a minor faerie court arrives in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]] demanding the return of a stolen season.

### Hook 22
A dancing light guides travelers to hidden feasts, then leaves them owing impossible favors.

### Hook 23
Two rival pixie envoys each present a valid treaty seal and accuse the other of being a glamour clone.

### Hook 24
A ring of mushrooms in [[landmarks/callidae/faerie-ring-hollow|Faerie Ring Hollow]] opens only for liars who confess first.

### Hook 25
Children in Moonwell Terraces begin speaking in perfect Sylvan after chasing a silver kite through fog.

### Hook 26
A satyr minstrel offers to end a border dispute if the party retrieves his true name from a locked harp.

### Hook 27
A fey courier collapses with an arrow of frozen moonlight and begs the party to finish her message route.

### Hook 28
A noble house wedding is interrupted when the bride's reflection steps out of a mirror and claims to be the legal spouse.

### Hook 29
Portals along [[landmarks/callidae/mistveil-path|Mistveil Path]] now lead to the same moonlit balcony regardless of entry point.

### Hook 30
A brownie guild in Rootmarket has gone on strike after someone replaced their oath-stone with common granite.

### Hook 31
A redcap trial demands mortal witnesses, but every witness ages one year for each hour in court.

### Hook 32
The faerie diplomat [[people/velyra-dawnhollow|Velyra Dawnhollow]] asks the party to expose which courtier is secretly Unseelie-backed.

### Hook 33
A stolen lullaby is causing sleepers to wander into fey crossings at midnight.

### Hook 34
A pact lantern that marks safe portal routes has been relit with shadowfire.

### Hook 35
Local sprites crown a random street vendor as "king for a night," and every order he gives becomes binding custom.

### Hook 36
An eladrin emissary refuses all negotiations until someone recovers her discarded spring face from a thorn maze.

### Hook 37
A vine bridge to a hidden court appears only during thunderstorms and vanishes with anyone still on it.

### Hook 38
A changeling child claims to remember tomorrow's council vote and begs for asylum.

### Hook 39
A faerie tax collector demands payment in treasured memories, starting with veterans of the last cavern war.

### Hook 40
A portal key made of woven moongrass has split in two, and each half now leads to hostile courts.

## Cavern Exploration

### Hook 41
Miners beneath [[regions/callidae-districts/rootmarket|Rootmarket]] opened a chamber where stone statues still bleed warm sap.

### Hook 42
An old patrol route used by [[people/vaelor-deepglen|Vaelor Deepglen]] has gone silent after three warning horns sounded underground.

### Hook 43
A map etched on crystal updates in real time, but only for tunnels no one remembers digging.

### Hook 44
The party is hired to escort lanternwrights repairing waystones that now point travelers deeper instead of home.

### Hook 45
A cavern lake reflects a starfield that does not match the sky above Callidae.

### Hook 46
Scouts find ruins of a pre-elf shrine where every inscription ends mid-sentence, as if the language was interrupted.

### Hook 47
A sinkhole swallowed a caravan and spat its wagons back up coated in living lichen armor.

### Hook 48
A collapsed archive reveals sealed tablets naming modern families as heirs to an extinct underrealm throne.

### Hook 49
Echoes in a narrow pass repeat conversations from one hour in the future.

### Hook 50
A burrowing basilisk has nested inside an abandoned cistern and turned the support columns brittle.

### Hook 51
An ore vein of moon-silver hums with druidic chants when touched by open flame.

### Hook 52
A relic lantern from the First Delvings lights only when pointed toward hidden graves.

### Hook 53
The Deep Wardens ask for help closing smuggler shafts that now connect to a haunted amphitheater.

### Hook 54
A fungal forest below the city has begun growing perfect copies of missing citizens.

### Hook 55
A bridge of root and bone appears overnight across a chasm no druid admits making.

### Hook 56
Prospectors found a sealed vault door engraved with symbols from [[regions/callidae-districts/astral-observatory|Astral Observatory]].

### Hook 57
A cave-in uncovered a sleeping construct carved from meteorite iron and druid-heartwood.

### Hook 58
Treasure hunters recovered a crown of translucent stone that attracts blind cave predators.

### Hook 59
A subterranean river has reversed flow and now carries offerings back toward the city.

### Hook 60
A lost survey team left chalk marks that move between walls whenever no one is watching.

## Astral Mysteries

### Hook 61
Star sages at [[regions/callidae-districts/astral-observatory|Astral Observatory]] detect a missing constellation that should govern harvest luck this season.

### Hook 62
A novice diviner sees the same falling star every night, always landing in a different district.

### Hook 63
The moonwell waters in [[regions/callidae-districts/moonwell-terraces|Moonwell Terraces]] rise on a cloudless noon and reveal a map of buried shrines.

### Hook 64
A comet cult claims the next alignment will open a gate to the first garden of the elves.

### Hook 65
A shipment of star charts arrives blank until viewed under blood-moon light.

### Hook 66
An astrolabe in the observatory rotates on its own and points to a cellar in Eldergrove.

### Hook 67
A dying astronomer leaves a coded will that can only be solved by arranging lanterns like planets.

### Hook 68
The constellation grove trees in [[landmarks/verdant-constellation-grove|Verdant Constellation Grove]] begin shedding luminous bark shaped like letters.

### Hook 69
A celestial conjunction causes all oath magic in the city to become literal for one night.

### Hook 70
Priests report that dawn arrives seven minutes late, but only inside sacred precincts.

### Hook 71
A star-metal shard grants true visions at the cost of one cherished memory per use.

### Hook 72
A phantom observatory appears above the city at midnight and mirrors every movement below.

### Hook 73
A child draws precise orbital diagrams of bodies unknown to current scholars.

### Hook 74
A ritual telescope lens cracked, and each shard now shows a different possible future.

### Hook 75
The party must guard a celestial summit where rival circles debate whether to block or welcome an omen.

### Hook 76
A meteor crater in nearby caverns contains seedlings that sprout in starlight but rot in sunlight.

### Hook 77
An eclipse rehearsal ceremony summons a being that insists the true eclipse already happened.

### Hook 78
Dreamers across Callidae wake with matching constellations inked on their palms.

### Hook 79
A timekeeping bell synced to the stars has begun tolling for people who are still alive.

### Hook 80
An old prophecy tablet was retranslated, shifting blame for a coming disaster onto a respected druid line.

### Hook 81
Black-thorn briars spread through public gardens and bloom only when someone lies nearby.

### Hook 82
A masked fey knight invites heroes to a midnight duel where every wound becomes a curse.

### Hook 83
Villagers vanish after following a sorrowful flute into the lower mushroom groves.

### Hook 84
A shadow dryad offers forbidden healing in exchange for years of the patient's future.

### Hook 85

### Hook 86

### Hook 87
A mirror pool once used for blessings now shows only scenes of betrayal.

### Hook 88
The party is asked to recover a stolen dawn-bell before dark fey ring it to seal night for a week.

### Hook 89
A cursed masque grants irresistible charm by day and monstrous hunger by night.

### Hook 90
A hunting pack of shadow hounds slips through weakened hedgewalls near Starlight Gardens.

### Hook 91
A corrupted treant uproots waymarkers and herds travelers toward an ambush vale.

### Hook 92
A noble patron sponsors anti-fey militias while secretly feeding names to Unseelie raiders.

### Hook 93
The dead in a small shrine rise peacefully each dusk and walk toward a forbidden gate.

### Hook 94
A glamoured carnival appears outside the city and trades joy for obedience.

### Hook 95
Night watch captains receive identical nightmares naming the same council member as a traitor.

### Hook 96
A plague of ink-black moths devours written contracts, leaving only signatures behind.

### Hook 97
The oldest moonwell has soured, and those healed there begin hearing commands from beneath the roots.

### Hook 98
An imprisoned hag offers the location of a shadow court embassy in return for a public pardon.

### Hook 99
A stolen relic from [[regions/callidae-districts/eldergrove|Eldergrove]] is being used to crown a pretender "night regent."

### Hook 100
At the next equinox, dark fey plan to invert every ward in Callidae unless five oathstones are purified before moonrise.

# Amn

## Overview
Amn is a wealthy mercantile power whose influence is tied to trade routes, coin, and commercial leverage.

## Identity
A realm known for pragmatism, profit-first politics, and powerful merchant interests.

## Geography
Position this nation along key caravan and coastal trade corridors in the western lands. Add local terrain details as needed for your campaign.

## Government
Rule is exercised by powerful merchant elites and their aligned institutions, with policy often shaped by economic priorities.

## Society
Social status often tracks with wealth, guild ties, and urban connections. Rural communities and frontier populations can have very different local norms.

## Economy
Trade, shipping, moneylending, and guild-controlled commerce drive Amn's power.

# Shadowdale

## Overview
A well-known dale settlement used as a local power and travel anchor within the [[nations-realms/dalelands|Dalelands]].

## Role in the Region
Serves as a meeting point for dale envoys, caravan traffic, and regional problem-solvers.

## Power and Institutions
- Dale council voices and respected local elders
- Militia captains and route wardens
- Temples and shrines with practical civic influence

## Campaign Use
Good for council politics, wilderness transition adventures, and missions triggered by inter-dale disputes.

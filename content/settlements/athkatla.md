# Athkatla

## Overview
Athkatla is the capital of [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]] and the realm's financial engine, where contracts, credit, and legal maneuvering can matter more than swords.

## Location
A major harbor-city on Amn's coast, Athkatla connects sea trade, inland caravan routes, and high-value finance networks.

## Power Structure
- formal influence from the [[factions/council-of-six|Council of Six]]
- strong behind-the-scenes pressure from counting houses and merchant dynasties
- district-level enforcement split across officials, private guards, and hired specialists

## Major Districts and Sites
- grand market and counting-house quarters
- dock wards where customs enforcement and smuggling intersect
- [[landmarks/waukeens-promenade|Waukeen's Promenade]] as a high-visibility trade and social hub

## Campaign Use
Use Athkatla for contract disputes, corruption investigations, smuggling crackdowns, faction negotiations, and deniable political work.

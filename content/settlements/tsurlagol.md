# Tsurlagol

## Overview
A wealthy caravan-port nexus in [[nations-realms/thesk|Thesk]] where sea captains, caravan factors, and information brokers all compete for leverage.

## Governance and Power
- Merchant costers and shipping syndicates heavily influence civic law.
- Harbor wardens and toll offices enforce order, but bribery pressure is constant.
- Outside envoys from [[nations-realms/impiltur|Impiltur]] and [[nations-realms/aglarond|Aglarond]] maintain active trade missions.

## Districts and Landmarks
- Coin Docks: bonded piers, customs courts, and auction halls.
- South Caravan Ring: beast yards, caravan compounds, and route insurers.
- Lantern Exchange: broker-houses, interpreters, and discreet contract rooms.

## Economy and Culture Hooks
- Core income comes from tariffs, caravan refinancing, and transshipment fees.
- Street culture rewards pragmatism: reputation matters more than birth rank.
- Shortages or war rumors can flip prices overnight and trigger violence.

## Adventure Use
- Recover a vanished ledger proving a tariff fraud scheme tied to several nations.
- Guard a negotiation between rival caravan syndicates before it turns into street war.
- Track a false-flag cargo that could spark a diplomatic crisis in the Easting Reach.

## Canon Notes
Tsurlagol is often treated as a major eastern trade city; exact internal politics vary by era and source.

# Delthuntle

## Overview
A river-linked interior town of [[nations-realms/aglarond|Aglarond]] that acts as a logistics hinge between forest communities and coastal ports.

## Governance and Power
- Local reeves and militia captains run day-to-day civic policy.
- Timber guilds and ferry contracts give merchant families quiet political weight.
- Border-watch liaisons monitor infiltration attempts linked to [[nations-realms/thay|Thay]].

## Districts and Landmarks
- River Quay: ferries, fish markets, and bonded storehouses.
- Green Ward: sawmills, bowyers, and militia training grounds.
- Old Shrine Hill: local protective shrines and civic oath gatherings.

## Economy and Culture Hooks
- Trade centers on timber, river freight, and crafted military supplies.
- Residents value practical defense, mutual aid, and suspicion of outside manipulation.
- Seasonal festivals blend rural custom with military musters.

## Adventure Use
- Investigate forged military writs diverting defense supplies.
- Escort a tribunal inspector through communities resisting crown oversight.
- Uncover a covert cell recruiting locals through debt and blackmail.

# Silverymoon

## Overview
A leading city of the North and a customary political center for [[nations-realms/luruar|Luruar (Silver Marches)]].

## Role in the Region
Acts as a diplomacy hub where member powers coordinate defense, trade policy, and frontier response.

## Power and Institutions
- Confederation delegates and treaty offices
- Mage colleges and civic scholars
- Merchant caravans tied to northern routes

## Campaign Use
Useful for alliance summits, escort missions, and disputes between frontier security and city priorities.

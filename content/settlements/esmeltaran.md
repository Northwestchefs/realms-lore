# Esmeltaran

## Overview
Esmeltaran is an inland Amnian city associated with administration, temple influence, and the management of nearby agricultural and trade hinterlands.

## Local Themes
- civic governance at some remove from Athkatla's dock politics
- temple-backed mediation in debt, inheritance, and market conflicts
- recurring friction between rural producers and urban contract enforcers

## Power Groups
- local magistrates and record-keepers
- faith institutions, especially [[faiths/waukeen|Waukeen]] and [[faiths/helm|Helm]]
- merchant representatives from [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]] and [[settlements/crimmor|Crimmor]]

## Campaign Use
Use Esmeltaran for legal dramas, temple arbitration, missing-ledger mysteries, and politics around food supply contracts.

## Canon Notes
Prominence and exact political weight can shift by era. Keep Esmeltaran as a useful inland counterbalance to port-focused Amnian stories.

# Trademeet

## Overview
Trademeet is a major inland market settlement in [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]], known for caravan exchange, sharp local bargaining traditions, and friction with metropolitan trade policy.

## Location
Trademeet sits on key overland trade corridors connecting Amn's coast-facing economy to interior caravan traffic.

## Civic Pattern
- market-first governance culture with strong merchant influence
- local leaders balancing tradition against pressure from Athkatlan investors
- law focused on market order, contracts, and caravan security

## Pressure Points
- guild and customs influence from [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]]
- rural producers resisting predatory contract terms
- caravan security demands tied to the [[regions/trade-way|Trade Way]]

## Campaign Use
Use Trademeet for caravan guard contracts, market arbitration, labor disputes, and stories about local autonomy versus central economic control.

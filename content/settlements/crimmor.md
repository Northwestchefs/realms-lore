# Crimmor

## Overview
Crimmor is one of Amn's key caravan and warehousing centers, where overland goods are sorted, taxed, insured, and redirected toward coastal ports.

## Role in Amn
- inland logistics node for [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]]
- crossroads pressure point between caravan masters, customs officials, and lenders
- frequent site of contract disputes and cargo fraud investigations

## Power Structure
- caravan guild brokers and warehouse syndicates
- magistrates influenced by Athkatlan capital
- hired guards and convoy companies enforcing private agreements

## Campaign Use
Use Crimmor for convoy escort arcs, disputed manifests, labor unrest, and courtroom-to-roadside investigation chains.

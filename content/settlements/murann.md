# Murann

## Overview
Murann is a strategically important Amnian port where legal cargo, maritime finance, and gray-market shipping all compete for control of the same docks.

## Location
On Amn's coast and tied to major sea-lanes, Murann is a gateway between Amn, [[nations-realms/tethyr|Tethyr]], and wider southern trade.

## Local Character
- hard-edged dock politics and constant tariff disputes
- investors with private marines and warehouse guards
- recurring tension between anti-smuggling mandates and profitable selective enforcement

## Power Groups
- port authorities aligned with Amnian merchant blocs
- shipping syndicates and insurers tied to [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]]
- covert operators linked to [[factions/shadow-thieves-athkatla|Shadow Thieves (Athkatla)]]

## Campaign Use
Use Murann for convoy escort arcs, counterfeit cargo investigations, customs corruption stories, and sea-lane sabotage plots.

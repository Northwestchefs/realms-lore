# House de'Lyr

## Overview
House de'Lyr is presented as a major mercantile-noble line in [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]], with influence in finance, shipping, and contract politics.

## Power Profile
- deep credit reserves and debt leverage tools
- stakeholdings in convoy, warehouse, and maritime ventures
- political access through council-aligned patron networks

## Campaign Use
Use House de'Lyr as patrons, financiers, antagonists, or kingmakers whose interests intersect with the [[factions/council-of-six|Council of Six]].

# Waukeen's Promenade

## Overview
A high-profile commercial and social complex in [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]], associated with elite trade culture, public prestige, and controlled access to wealth.

## Why It Matters
- neutral-looking ground for high-stakes negotiations
- dense overlap of legal commerce, espionage, and social signaling
- ideal location for theft, blackmail, and reputation warfare plots

## Campaign Use
Use it for meeting scenes, contract exchanges, surveillance missions, and sudden public incidents that force political responses.

## Canon Notes
Exact layout, ownership, and prominence vary by source emphasis. Keep the site as a recognizable symbol of Amnian mercantile power.

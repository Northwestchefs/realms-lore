# Yuirwood

## Overview
A deep, old forest of [[nations-realms/aglarond|Aglarond]] associated with ancient sites, local guardianship traditions, and uncertain magical boundaries.

## Why It Matters in Play
- Ideal for patrol missions, missing-person searches, and ruin-delving with political consequences.
- Local communities treat parts of the wood as spiritually and historically sensitive ground.
- Foreign agents often exploit rumors about hidden relics to cover espionage activity.

## Adventure Angles
- A border patrol vanishes while tracking smugglers using old woodland paths.
- Competing scholars hire the party to secure an ancient site before looters arrive.
- A seasonal ward fails, letting hostile creatures push toward settled roads.

# Thaymount

## Overview
A volcanic plateau associated with the power core of [[nations-realms/thay|Thay]], feared across the East as a symbol of arcane militarization.

## Why It Matters in Play
- Serves as a high-threat source of magical logistics, elite agents, and strategic fear.
- Rumors tied to Thaymount can destabilize diplomacy far beyond Thayan borders.
- Expeditions toward the plateau can anchor espionage, extraction, or sabotage arcs.

## Adventure Angles
- A defector claims proof of a new arcane weapon program and needs escort west.
- Rival factions hire the party to verify contradictory intelligence about activity on the plateau.
- A ritual side effect causes magical fallout in distant settlements along trade routes.

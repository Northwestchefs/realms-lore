# Faerie Ring Hollow

## Description
A hollowed grove of naturally formed rings where fey crossings briefly thin the veil between realms.

## Atmosphere
Playful whispers and drifting lights shift into uneasy silence after nightfall.

## Notable Creatures
- Faerie dragons
- Star moth spirits
- Elusive fey beasts

## Story Hooks
- A ring remains open too long, drawing hostile fey into the gardens.
- A missing child returns with impossible memories from the other side.
- Druids seek mediators for a tense pact renewal with local fey.

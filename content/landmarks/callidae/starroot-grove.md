# Starroot Grove

## Description
An old growth of deep-rooted magical flora whose roots absorb and redistribute ambient celestial energy.

## Atmosphere
The air thrums with low, harmonic resonance as root-veins pulse with silver light.

## Notable Creatures
- Crystal beetles
- Star moth spirits
- Faerie dragons

## Story Hooks
- Illegal harvesters tunnel in from below to steal mature starroots.
- A blighted root cluster begins dimming nearby crystal lanterns.
- Druids need escorts for a dangerous midnight pruning rite.

# The Elder Greenhouse

## Description
A sealed living greenhouse managed by elder druids to cultivate delicate, high-magic plants.

## Atmosphere
Warm and humid under crystalline panes, with layered scents of sap, pollen, and old earth.

## Notable Creatures
- Luminous frogs
- Crystal beetles
- Tiny faerie dragon hatchlings

## Story Hooks
- A protected specimen is dying despite constant care.
- An apprentice is accused of swapping rare seedlings with counterfeits.
- A surge of wild magic causes overnight, dangerous overgrowth.

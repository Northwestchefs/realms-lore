# The Hidden Warren

## Description
A concealed network of burrows and root chambers where small sacred creatures shelter during dangerous seasons.

## Atmosphere
Close, warm tunnels lined with soft moss and crystal fragments that glow like embers.

## Notable Creatures
- Baby unicorns
- Luminous frogs
- Crystal beetles

## Story Hooks
- The warren's guardians report strange digging from deep, unknown tunnels.
- A rare clutch of crystal beetles fails to hatch on schedule.
- Raiders attempt to map the warren to target protected creatures.

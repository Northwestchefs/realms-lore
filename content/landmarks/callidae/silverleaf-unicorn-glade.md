# Silverleaf Unicorn Glade

## Description
A protected meadow beneath towering silverleaf boughs where young unicorns are raised under druidic watch.

## Atmosphere
Quiet, bright, and reverent, with shimmering pollen and soft hoofprints glowing in the soil.

## Notable Creatures
- Baby unicorns
- Luminous frogs
- Crystal beetles

## Story Hooks
- A foal has gone missing, and tracks vanish at a ring of broken crystals.
- Poachers seek a unicorn shedding said to cure any curse.
- A frightened herd reacts to a hidden corruption spreading in nearby roots.

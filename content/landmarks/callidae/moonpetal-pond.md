# Moonpetal Pond

## Description
A moon-bright basin where rare moonpetal lilies bloom and druids perform healing and divination rites.

## Atmosphere
Still water mirrors star-crystal light, while mist curls low across the shoreline.

## Notable Creatures
- Star moth spirits
- Luminous frogs
- Faerie dragons

## Story Hooks
- The lilies refuse to open before a critical healing ceremony.
- A pond spirit demands restitution for stolen moonpetal cuttings.
- Strange ripples reveal visions of an approaching fey incursion.

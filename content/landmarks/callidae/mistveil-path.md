# Mistveil Path

## Description
A winding pilgrimage route where drifting enchanted fog veils sacred markers and hidden branch trails.

## Atmosphere
Muted footsteps, cool damp air, and sudden clearings lit by crystal stars.

## Notable Creatures
- Faerie dragons
- Luminous frogs
- Star moth spirits

## Story Hooks
- Pilgrims keep reappearing at the trailhead with no memory of their journey.
- A corrupted fey court uses the mist to mislead travelers.
- Ancient waystones reactivate during a rare celestial conjunction.

# Myth Drannor

## Overview
A legendary ruined city associated with lost magic, ancient conflict, and high-risk expedition play.

## Location
Placed in the broader Dalelands sphere for campaign routing between forest travel and settled corridors.

## Why It Matters
- Prime site for relic recovery and dangerous lore.
- Magnet for rival expeditions and secret patrons.
- A pressure point where old wards, monsters, and treasure hunters collide.

## Access and Travel Notes
Parties usually stage from nearby dale settlements and should expect contested approach routes.

## Canon Notes
Its status changes across timelines and sources. Treat current condition and occupancy as campaign-tunable.

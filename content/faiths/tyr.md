# Tyr

## Overview
Tyr is broadly associated with justice, law, duty, and righteous judgment.

## Public Role
Temples, judges, and knightly adherents are often involved in mediation, legal process, and public oaths.

## Regional Footprint
- **Sea of Fallen Stars courts:** Temple advocates and magistrates often help keep charter law consistent between ports.
- **Cormyrean circuit justice:** Tyran judges travel between settlements when local power blocs try to bury cases.

## Key NPCs in the Realms
- [[people/justicar-maelis-durn|Justicar Maelis Durn]] — Cormyr circuit-judge based from [[settlements/suzail|Suzail]] in the [[regions/sea-of-fallen-stars|Sea of Fallen Stars]].

## Campaign Use
Supports trials, investigations, moral dilemmas, and conflicts between strict law and practical mercy.

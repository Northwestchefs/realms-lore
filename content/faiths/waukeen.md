# Waukeen

## Overview
Waukeen is commonly associated with trade, wealth, bargains, and mercantile ambition.

## Public Role
Temples and clergy often act as brokers, lenders, arbitrators, and sponsors of market confidence.

## In Amn
Waukeen's institutions are especially visible in [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]], where legitimacy in trade can matter as much as coin itself.

## Campaign Use
Useful in stories involving contracts, debt, trade disputes, and ethical conflict around profit and obligation.

# Waukeen

## Overview
Waukeen is commonly associated with trade, wealth, bargains, and mercantile ambition.

## Public Role
Temples and clergy often act as brokers, lenders, arbitrators, and sponsors of market confidence.

## Regional Footprint
- **Sea of Fallen Stars commerce:** Temple factors influence risk, insurance rates, and cargo legitimacy.
- **Amnian markets:** Waukeen's institutions stay visible where law, lending, and civic status are tied to successful trade.

## Key NPCs in the Realms
- [[people/factor-irielle-vann|Factor Irielle Vann]] — Temple-broker in [[settlements/selgaunt|Selgaunt]] with links into [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]] and the wider [[regions/sea-of-fallen-stars|Sea of Fallen Stars]].

## Campaign Use
Useful in stories involving contracts, debt, trade disputes, and ethical conflict around profit and obligation.

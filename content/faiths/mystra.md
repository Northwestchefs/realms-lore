# Mystra

## Overview
Mystra is widely revered as a central divine figure of magic, making her faith influential wherever arcane practice intersects with civic life.

## Public Presence
Temples and shrines often appear near mage guilds, libraries, academies, and cities where magical services affect daily life.

## Regional Footprint
- **Sword Coast arcane corridors:** Mystic shrines support ward maintenance, relic containment, and magical emergency response.
- **Northern alliances:** Temple arcanists often bridge local governments, guild mages, and independent scholars.

## Key NPCs in the Realms
- [[people/magister-selene-dawnscript|Magister Selene Dawnscript]] — Mystra's arcane troubleshooter in [[settlements/silverymoon|Silverymoon]], active across the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]].

## Political Relevance
Rulers and factions may seek temple legitimacy, magical counsel, or mediation when arcane incidents threaten public stability.

## Campaign Use
Useful for magical disasters, artifact custody disputes, arcane ethics debates, and alliances between scholars and civic leaders.

# Tempus

## Overview
A widely known war faith focused on courage, discipline, and the brutal reality of armed conflict.

## Portfolio and Public Role
Battle, martial honor, and the acceptance that war can reshape politics faster than treaties.

## Worship Patterns
- Soldiers and mercenaries seeking resolve before battle
- Commanders wanting blessing without strict moral doctrine
- Communities on dangerous borders

## Institutions and Influence
Shrines are common near barracks, border roads, and mustering grounds rather than large centralized hierarchies.

# Helm

## Overview
Helm is associated with vigilance, guardianship, duty, and the disciplined protection of people and institutions.

## Public Role
In trade-heavy regions like [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]], Helmite influence is often visible in guard culture, caravan protection, oathkeeping, and temple-backed security contracts.

## Regional Footprint
- **Trade Way security web:** Watch shrines coordinate escort timing, ward-post signaling, and convoy standards.
- **Amnian civic defense:** Helmite veterans train militias and enforce duty-first command culture.

## Key NPCs in the Realms
- [[people/shieldcaptain-oth-brammar|Shieldcaptain Oth Brammar]] — Helmite convoy marshal in [[settlements/esmeltaran|Esmeltaran]] along the [[regions/trade-way|Trade Way]].

## Campaign Use
Useful in stories about sworn duty, conflicting orders, public safety versus profit, and defensive stands under political pressure.
